About Us

Who is Winka Tech Solutions

We are a forward-thinking tech company based in Kenya, proudly owned and operated by Kenyans. Established in 2020 under the laws and constitution of Kenya, Winka Tech Solutions specializes in developing and maintaining websites, management systems, and innovative Point Of Sale (POS) systems. Since our inception, we have empowered countless businesses by offering digital solutions that streamline operations, enhance efficiency, and double productivity.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses in Kenya with seamless and efficient digital tools that elevate productivity.

Our Core Values

Transparency, Customer Focus, Integrity, Accountability, Efficiency.

Our Catchline

Together we transform.
